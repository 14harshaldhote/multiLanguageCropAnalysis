About This Tool

This crop disease detection tool uses advanced machine learning models to identify common plant diseases. Simply upload a clear image of a plant leaf, and our system will:

  1. Detect if the leaf is healthy or diseased
  2. Identify the specific disease affecting the plant
  3. Provide information about the cause, prevention, and treatment options

Results can be displayed in multiple languages to help farmers worldwide.
